Minecraft Server Season History

Season 1

Not much is known about season 1 as it was one of our very early Minecraft seasons. This season was on the Minecraft 1.16 Nether update on realms.

Season 2

Ahh... our beloved season 2. This was the most popular season among my friends making it one of our most active seasons. This season took place on the 1.16 update on a realms server. Our main base was in a beautiful jungle and we all had our houses connected with a network of bridges. This season also had the first appearance of Kozland which is the most loved area on the server.
